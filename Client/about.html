Mission

The Center for Admission and Scholarship office will provide venues and opportunities to making UMak education, that is of quality and relevance, accessible to financially challenged but deserving students of Makati and of the country. It will continually deliver effective and efficient services related to admission, scholarship and financial assistance to its customers.

Vision

In keeping with UMak’s vision and mission, the Center for Admission and Scholarship links the University, its programs, stakeholders, and infrastructure with the local government of the City of Makati to making quality and relevant education accessible to financially challenged but deserving students of Makati and of the country.

Background

The University through the Center for Admission and Scholarship in collaboration with the Office of the Vice President for Academic Affairs and the Office of the Vice President for Student Services and Community Development is responsible for the development and strategy to attract the best and deserving young Makatizens to partake of UMak’s quality education through its various academic programs. Herein-stated are the policies and procedures on admission and enrollment for incoming students.

The University of Makati (UMak) is a public, locally funded university of the local government of Makati. It is envisioned as the primary instrument where university education and industry training programs interface to mold Makati and non-Makati youth into productive citizens and IT-enabled professionals who are exposed to cutting-edge technology in their areas of specialization. UMak is the final stage of Makati City's integrated primary level to tertiary level educational system that enables its less privileged citizens to compete for job opportunities in various businesses and industries.
